# Jansori(잔소리) Assistant

An AI-powered personal assistant project focused on habit management, routine building, and realistic daily accountability.

잔소리 is inspired by the feeling of having someone quietly watching over your daily life — reminding you to sleep on time, exercise, drink water, stay organized, and maintain healthy routines.

Not as a productivity coach, but more like a slightly annoying, caring companion.

## Goals

The goal of this project is to explore:

- Personal AI assistant systems
- Long-term memory and habit tracking
- LLM-based conversations and personality design
- Notification and reminder systems
- Human-centered AI interaction
- Physical AI companion concepts

This project is also a personal learning journey to better understand:

- AI/LLM application development
- Agent architecture
- Voice and conversational systems
- Automation and workflow design
- Full-stack prototyping
